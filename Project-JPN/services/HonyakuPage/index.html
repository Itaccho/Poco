翻訳（ほんやく）の練習（れんしゅう）をする必要性（ひつようせい）とは
Why is it necessary to do practice of translation?

In order to be fluent in Japanese, the first thing you have to do is to practice translation from your first language into Japanese. I've seen many students who cannot come up with words they want to tell, although they already know what those words mean.

This situation shows that you aren't used to using Japanese.
